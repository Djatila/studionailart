# Requirements Document

## Introduction

Modernização da landing page do SaaS de agendamento "Studio Nail Art", focando em alta conversão, design sofisticado e experiência do usuário otimizada para nail designers e manicures, mantendo a identidade visual atual com gradiente roxo-rosa-magenta.

## Glossary

- **Landing_Page**: Página principal de vendas do SaaS Studio Nail Art para nail designers e manicures
- **CTA_Button**: Botão de chamada para ação (Call-to-Action)
- **Hero_Section**: Seção principal no topo da página
- **Social_Proof**: Elementos que demonstram credibilidade e confiança
- **Conversion_Rate**: Taxa de conversão de visitantes em leads/clientes
- **Mobile_First**: Abordagem de design priorizando dispositivos móveis
- **Micro_Interactions**: Pequenas animações e feedbacks visuais
- **Trust_Signals**: Elementos que transmitem confiança e profissionalismo

## Requirements

### Requirement 1

**User Story:** Como uma nail designer visitante, eu quero uma experiência visual impactante e profissional, para que eu confie na qualidade do serviço oferecido.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a user loads the page, THE Landing_Page SHALL display a modern hero section with improved visual hierarchy and typography
2. WHILE browsing the page, THE Landing_Page SHALL maintain consistent premium visual aesthetics throughout all sections
3. THE Landing_Page SHALL implement sophisticated micro-interactions and hover effects for enhanced user engagement
4. WHERE visual elements are displayed, THE Landing_Page SHALL use high-quality graphics and refined spacing
5. THE Landing_Page SHALL preserve the existing purple-pink-magenta gradient color scheme

### Requirement 2

**User Story:** Como uma potencial cliente, eu quero entender rapidamente o valor da solução, para que eu possa tomar uma decisão informada sobre o cadastro.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Landing_Page SHALL present a clear value proposition within the first 3 seconds of page load
2. WHEN viewing the benefits section, THE Landing_Page SHALL display quantifiable benefits and outcomes
3. THE Landing_Page SHALL include compelling before/after scenarios that resonate with the target audience
4. WHERE pain points are mentioned, THE Landing_Page SHALL immediately follow with corresponding solutions
5. THE Landing_Page SHALL use persuasive copywriting techniques to increase conversion intent

### Requirement 3

**User Story:** Como usuária mobile, eu quero uma experiência otimizada para dispositivos móveis, para que eu possa navegar facilmente pelo meu smartphone.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Landing_Page SHALL implement mobile-first responsive design principles
2. WHEN accessed on mobile devices, THE Landing_Page SHALL maintain fast loading times under 3 seconds
3. THE Landing_Page SHALL ensure all interactive elements are touch-friendly with minimum 44px touch targets
4. WHILE scrolling on mobile, THE Landing_Page SHALL provide smooth scrolling performance
5. THE Landing_Page SHALL optimize images and assets for mobile bandwidth constraints

### Requirement 4

**User Story:** Como visitante interessada, eu quero ver provas sociais e credibilidade, para que eu me sinta segura em fornecer meus dados.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Landing_Page SHALL display authentic testimonials with photos and specific results
2. THE Landing_Page SHALL include trust signals such as user count, success metrics, or certifications
3. WHEN viewing social proof elements, THE Landing_Page SHALL present them in visually appealing formats
4. THE Landing_Page SHALL incorporate subtle animations to draw attention to credibility elements
5. WHERE testimonials are shown, THE Landing_Page SHALL include diverse representation of nail designers and manicures

### Requirement 5

**User Story:** Como potencial usuária, eu quero CTAs claros e persuasivos, para que eu saiba exatamente qual ação tomar.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Landing_Page SHALL feature prominent, contrasting CTA_Buttons throughout the page
2. WHEN hovering over CTA elements, THE Landing_Page SHALL provide immediate visual feedback
3. THE Landing_Page SHALL use action-oriented, benefit-focused CTA copy
4. THE Landing_Page SHALL implement strategic CTA placement based on user journey mapping
5. WHERE multiple CTAs exist, THE Landing_Page SHALL maintain consistent styling and messaging

### Requirement 6

**User Story:** Como nail designer ou manicure profissional, eu quero ver demonstrações visuais do produto, para que eu entenda como a ferramenta funcionará no meu negócio.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Landing_Page SHALL include interactive product demonstrations or enhanced mockups
2. WHEN viewing product features, THE Landing_Page SHALL show realistic usage scenarios
3. THE Landing_Page SHALL implement progressive disclosure of feature information
4. THE Landing_Page SHALL use visual storytelling to explain the user journey
5. WHERE product benefits are listed, THE Landing_Page SHALL accompany them with supporting visuals

### Requirement 7

**User Story:** Como visitante, eu quero uma experiência de carregamento rápida e fluida, para que eu não abandone a página por lentidão.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Landing_Page SHALL achieve a PageSpeed Insights score above 90 for mobile and desktop
2. THE Landing_Page SHALL implement lazy loading for images and non-critical content
3. WHEN animations are triggered, THE Landing_Page SHALL maintain 60fps performance
4. THE Landing_Page SHALL optimize all assets for web delivery
5. THE Landing_Page SHALL implement efficient caching strategies for repeat visitors
